Who We Are

Established in 2012, Quanzhou Honglinkai New Material Co., Ltd. is a trusted manufacturer specializing in high-quality adhesive products, located in the culturally rich city of Quanzhou, East China. With over 20 years of expertise in the tape market, we have built a strong reputation for our innovative Mouth Tape and Nasal Strips, designed to meet the diverse needs of global customers. Our 5,000 m² manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced production machinery, including precision coating, rewinding, slitting, and packing systems, ensuring efficient and consistent product quality. Every item we produce is backed by international certifications, reflecting our commitment to excellence and safety. Beyond manufacturing, we take pride in our dedicated after-sales service team, which provides reliable support and technical assistance to ensure complete customer satisfaction. At Quanzhou Honglinkai, we combine cutting-edge technology, rigorous quality control, and a customer-centric approach to deliver innovative adhesive solutions that make a difference in people’s lives.
