Premium Mouth Tapes

Mouth Tapes not only relieve dry, cracked or rough lips, but also improve sleep quality for people with snoring or mouth breathing problems. Perfect for health and wellness brands, Amazon sellers and custom OEM/ODM projects.

  • Medical-Grade: Soft and breathable, suitable for sensitive skin, commonly used for functional mouth tape.
  • Water-Soluble or Biodegradable Materials: Eco-friendly mouth tape emphasizing sustainability and skin-friendliness.
  • Adhesive Glue: Used to ensure the tape adheres securely, usually a hypoallergenic, residue-free medical adhesive or water-soluble glue, easy to remove without damaging the skin.
