HLK 6-In-1 Nourishing Silicone Mouth Tape - Vitamin, Aloe, COQ10 & Collagen Infused

Experience better sleep and improved lip health with HLK’s innovative Infused Mouth Tape. Made from premium silicone, this high-quality tape is enhanced with six powerful ingredients—vitamin-rich aloe vera, skin-loving collagen, and rejuvenating CoQ10—working synergistically to nourish and hydrate your lips while you sleep.Its gentle, residue-free adhesive encourages natural nose breathing, helps reduce snoring, and prevents lip dryness overnight. Our unique infusion technology ensures the beneficial nutrients are slowly released throughout the night, leaving you with softer, more supple lips by morning.Perfect for anyone looking to boost sleep quality while caring for lip wellness, the hypoallergenic silicone is comfortable for extended wear and easy to remove. Making it a convenient addition to your nightly self-care routine.
