Wholesale Nasal Strips

Nasal Strips provide an effective, drug-free solution to improve airflow and reduce nasal congestion. By gently lifting the nasal passages, they help relieve snoring, enhance breathing during physical activity, and support post-surgery recovery. Perfect for health and wellness brands, Amazon sellers, and custom OEM/ODM projects.

  • Materials: Non-woven Fabric, Transparent PE, Cotton Satin.
  • Adhesive: Henkel’s medical-grade, hypoallergenic adhesive.
  • Enhancements: Customizable with refreshing scent such as lavender, eucalyptus, and mint.
